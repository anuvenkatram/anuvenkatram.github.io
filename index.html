Creative. Open. Willing to Learn.

Anulekha. Meaning: “A person who loves to write.”

It always amazes me how my parents gave me the perfect name. How so? For one, I graduated from Allegheny College with a Bachelor’s Degree in English and minors in Spanish and Economics. Secondly, I spend my free time contributing articles to an inspiration hub for millennial women called Her Agenda.

But here’s the interesting part—writing is my hobby! Entrepreneurship is my passion.

As a student at Allegheny College, I cofounded a bubble tea startup. After graduating, I joined Startup Institute's 8-week program as a Technical Marketing student. Over there, I was exposed to the NYC startup environment and was the Project Manager for my Partner Project team. More importantly, I discovered that there were so many things out there to learn! From HTML to Google Analytics, my interest was hooked.

More recently, I participated in NASA’s International SpaceApps Challenge, where my team, Senti8, was nominated to compete internationally.

My experiences have made me realize that in order to be successful, one needs to be AWESOME, i.e. Adaptable, Willing to learn, Entrepreneurial, Self-Disciplined, Organized, and Motivated. I aim to apply these skills in my professional development as I pursue a career in Product Management.
