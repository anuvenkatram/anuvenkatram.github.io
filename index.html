Recent Grad, Entrepreneur, and Social Do-gooder

My name is Anulekha, and I recently graduated from Allegheny College with a Bachelor of Arts degree in English and minors in Spanish and Economics. Currently, I am involved with the Technical Marketing Track at Startup Institute New York, an 8-week career accelerator that equips career changers and recent graduates with the skills necessary to make an impact on a startup company.

While a student at Allegheny College, I was one of the founders of the startup, Laughing Buddha Bubble Tea, LLC. Born out of Allegheny College's Entrepreneurship II class, Laughing Buddha Bubble Tea provided its college community with a fun, innovative drink and donated half of its proceeds towards student scholarships. In addition to being a startup founder, I was involved with my campus' interfaith movement, which aimed to bridge the gap between various faith and non-faith organization through action and dialogue. These experiences made me realize two things: my interest in social entrepreneurship and desire to keep learning.

As a persistent, creative individual, I am ready to embark on a career that intellectually engages me and pushes me to my limits. I would love to be able to work in an environment where I can continue to learn new things, help my community (be it on a local, national, or global scale), work with an awesome team, and have fun.
