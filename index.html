About me

A recent graduate of Allegheny College, I am ready to embark on a career that intellectually engages me and pushes me to my limits. I hold a Bachelor of Arts degree in English with minors in Spanish and Economics.

While a student at Allegheny College, I served as a leader for three student organizations, worked as a Resident Advisor, and was one of the founding members of the start-up, Laughing Buddha Bubble Tea, LLC. I was also recognized for my role in the creation of the Prayer and Meditation Retreat (a centrally located residential building that was converted into a prayer and meditation space for Buddhist, Muslim, and Hindu students) by receiving the Cornerstone Award.

As a persistent, creative individual, I am open to any opportunity that gives me the chance to exercise my creativity, build relationships, make an impact on my community, and allows me to continue learning new skills.

Currently, I'm excited to be a Technical Marketing student at Startup Institute.

What am I looking for?

I would love to be able to work in an environment where I can continue to learn new things, help my community (be it on a local, national, or global scale), work with an awesome team, and have fun. Personally, I believe that the best way to work is to have fun doing it!

What do I do for fun?

In my free time, I'm an avid reader, writer, music enthusiast, and zumba-loving fitness nut. I hope to go skydiving someday.
